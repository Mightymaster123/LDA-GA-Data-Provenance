Welcome

Thank you for visiting the Western Pennsylvania National Wild Animal Orphanage website.

Overview:
Each year the PA Game Commission and other law enforcement agencies rescue a number of exotic felines from inadequate or abusive homes and businesses. Unfortunately, these agencies are not able to care for these animals and there aren't many places willing to take in a wayward lion. Zoo's aren't always interested, and neither is the gang from the old pride. Once an animal has been tainted by human contact, an orphanage is usually its last hope.
