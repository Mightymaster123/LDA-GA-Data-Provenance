Former Labor MP Karen Overington dies

Updated August 12, 2011 09:10:45

Photo: Karen Overington has passed away aged 59 (Broken Daze85, Flickr Creative Commons)
Map: Ballarat West 3350

Karen Overington, the former member of state Parliament for Ballarat West, has died after a battle with ill health.

She was 59.

Ms Overington was a member of the Labor Party and held the seat for more than 10 years before retiring from politics last year.

In a statement, Opposition Leader Daniel Andrews described her as a dedicated mother, wife and grandmother who was a "faithful servant" to the people of Ballarat West.

Sitting member Sharon Knight praised Ms Overington for standing up for disadvantaged members of the community.

She said Ms Overington had left a lasting legacy and would be sadly missed.
