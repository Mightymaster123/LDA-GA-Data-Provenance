Hague: Syria military action 'not remote possibility'

1 August 2011 Last updated at 11:29 Help

The Foreign Secretary has condemned the Syrian authorities' crackdown on protesters on Sunday, which activists say has left more than 130 dead.

William Hague has called for stronger international pressure on the Syrian regime.

But Mr Hague told Today on BBC Radio 4 that military action against Syria was "not a remote possibility".
