Man dies after empty van rolls into him        28/3/13

A local man has been killed in a tragic accident after his parked van rolled into him today.

It is understood the man had been driving but stepped out of vehicle briefly at Ford Terrace in Creagorry, Benbecula late morning.

He suffered fatal injuries when the empty van rolled off and collided into him.

The emergency services were alerted at 11.40am. Ambulance, police and Benbecula fire crew attended at the scene.

The road policing unit from Dingwall are currently conducting a full investigation into the circumstances.
