Neymar Jr signs contract extension to 2021

www.fcbarcelona.com - 21/10/2016 | 15:50

The Brazilian put pen-to-paper at FC Barcelona along with club president Josep Maria Bartomeu

Neymar Jr signs through to 2021 | MIGUEL RUIZ - FCB

Neymar Jr extended his deal with FC Barcelona on Friday afternoon, putting pen-to-paper on his contract at the club’s facilities in the presence of president Josep Maria Bartomeu.

As the club announced, Neymar has signed a five-year deal which will keep him at Barça until 30 June 2021.

9 titles and 91 goals

The Brazilian has won nine titles since joining FC Barcelona in the summer of 2013: 2 Leagues(2014/15 and 2015/16), 2 Copa del Rey (2014/15 and 2015/16), 1 Champions League (2014/15), 2 Spanish Super Cups (2013 and 2016), 1 European Super Cup (2015) and 1 Club World Cup (2015). He has played 150 matches with the first team and has scored 91 goals in all competitions.
