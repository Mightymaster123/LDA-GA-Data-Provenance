connected to thursday s bombings and called the incident a tragedy a day earlier the police commissioner said the man was directly linked to thursday s attacks the police statement said we are now satisfied that he was not connected with the incidents of thursday 21st july 2005 for somebody to lose their life in such circumstances is a tragedy and one that the metropolitan police service regrets the man was followed when he left a flat that had been under surveillence he did not obey instructions from police not in uniform as he ran onto a tube train at the station eyewitnesses said
