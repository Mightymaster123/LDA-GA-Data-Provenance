FIFA bans Sergio Jadue for life - Wikinews, the free news source
friday may 6 2016 today the ethics committee of fifa the international federation of association football sanctioned the former president of the chilean football association anfp and former vice president of conmebol the south american football confederation sergio jadue with a lifetime ban from participating in any activity to do with football from december 4 sergio jadue was investigated for having participated in a fifa corruption and fraud scandal he admitted his involvement fifa in its statement today upheld the life ban for jadue and cited violation of fifa code of ethics articles 13 rules of behavior 15 disloyalty 18 duty to cooperate 19 conflict of interest and 21 bribery and corruption the same sanction also fell on former member of the executive committee of fifa former
