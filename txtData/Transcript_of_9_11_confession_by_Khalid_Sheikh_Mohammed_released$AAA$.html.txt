11 operation from a to z  i was the operational director for sheikh usama bin laden for the organizing planning follow up and execution of the 9 11 operation under the military commander sheikh abu said the transcript contains a list of 31 items mohammed wholly or partially confesses to these include alleged al qaeda attacks such as the bombing of a night club in bali and the attempt by richard
